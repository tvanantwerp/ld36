Welcome to the WMATA Metrorail Repair Dispatch System!

The metro system is constantly in need of repairs. It's your job to send repair crews to stations experiencing problems.

When a station on your map changes color, it means they've reported an issue. The log at the bottom of your screen will tell you more about which station reported what kind of problem. You can click that station to dispatch a repair crew.

Stations undergoing repairs will be green on your map. When they change from green back to neutral yellow, repairs are complete!

You only have 5 repairmen available, so choose carefully where to send them first! Stations in the center of DC can affect multiple lines, and repairs there are paramount!

Problems in the metro system will reduce ridership along the different lines. You can see the service level of each line at the bottom of your screen.

If the entire system has 0% capacity for too long, you will be FIRED!

Haha, just kidding! We don't fire people. But seriously, it will be game over and you'll have to try again.
